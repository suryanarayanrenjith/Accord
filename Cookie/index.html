Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Accord IND Services ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or to enhance the user experience by providing information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons, including:

  • To enhance the functionality of our website.
  • To analyze how our website is used and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.
  • To remember your preferences and settings on our website.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies may be set through various channels, including social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram.
